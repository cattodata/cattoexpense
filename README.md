# CattoExpense 🐱💸

A privacy-first personal expense tracker — upload your bank statement and get instant spending insights, charts, and AI coaching. Everything runs in your browser; no data is ever sent to a server.

**🔗 [cattodata.github.io/cattoexpense](https://cattodata.github.io/cattoexpense)**

## Supported Banks

> ⚠️ Currently supports select Australian banks only.

Supported statement formats (CSV / PDF):
- Commonwealth Bank (CommBank)
- ANZ
- NAB
- Westpac

Support for more banks and countries is planned.

## Features

- Upload CSV or PDF bank statements
- Automatic transaction categorisation & subcategories
- Spending charts (pie, bar, area)
- Month-over-month comparison
- AI-powered coaching & insights
- Export filtered transactions
- 100% client-side — your data never leaves your device
